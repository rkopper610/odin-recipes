Fosolia (Ethiopian Green Beans)

History of the dish

Fosolia or "Green-beans" is a fantastic side dish in Ethiopian cuisine. It is a kind of stir fry made with green beans, carrots and potatos.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • 1 Large red onion
  • 4 Garlic cloves
  • A 'bag' of green beans
  • One potato
  • One medium carrot

Steps to cook

  1. Heat oil in a medium pot
  2. Dice Onion and Garlic
  3. Add diced onion to heated oil
  4. Cook until translucent
  5. While onions are cooking, remove ends of beans and break in half
  6. slice potatoes and carrots into sticks
  7. Add potato and carrot and cover pot to steam the vegatables
  8. Add Garlic and salt
  9. Let on high, stirring occasionally
  10. Spoon onto injera
  11. Enjoy!
