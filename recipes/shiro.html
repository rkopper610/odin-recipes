Shiro

History of the dish

Shiro can be considered signiture dish in Ethiopian cuisine. It is made using chick pea flower and an untold number of spices. If you can get your hands on some shiro powder and Injera(Ethiopian flat bread), and can follow these simple directions then you are in for a treat.

Ingredients

  • Oil
  • 1 Large red onion
  • 4 Garlic cloves
  • One tomato
  • Shiro Powder
  • Salt
  • Jalapeno pepper
  • Injera
  • Water
  • Kibe (Ethiopian Butter) - optional

Steps to cook

  1. Heat oil in a medium pot
  2. Dice Onion and Garlic
  3. Add diced onion to heated oil
  4. Cook until translucent
  5. While onions are cooking puree tomato
  6. Add tomato and water
  7. Stir in Shiro powder
  8. Add Garlic and Butter (optional)
  9. Let simmer for 40 minutes with minimal stirring
  10. Spoon onto injera and top with diced jalpenos
  11. Enjoy!
